School Gardens

Our gardening school offers hands-on learning experiences designed to connect students with nature. Through collaborative garden projects, students gain practical skills in sustainable gardening, fostering an understanding of eco-friendly practices while growing their own plants. Join us to cultivate knowledge, teamwork, and a love for the environment!

Seasonal Produce

Our gardening school offers guidance on growing and harvesting seasonal produce, teaching students to work with nature’s rhythms for the best yields. We focus on cultivating a variety of fruits, vegetables, and herbs suited to each season, helping students gain skills in sustainable, climate-friendly gardening. Through seasonal planting workshops, we empower our community to enjoy fresh, nutritious produce year-round directly from their own gardens.

Our Trips

At our gardening school’s farms, students gain hands-on experience growing seasonal produce, learning sustainable practices directly in the field. Our farms serve as living classrooms where students explore every stage of planting, nurturing, and harvesting fresh produce. This immersive experience fosters a deep understanding of farm-to-table practices and the importance of eco-friendly agriculture.

Organic Compost

We emphasize the use of organic compost in our gardening practices, teaching students to recycle natural materials to enrich the soil and support plant health. Our composting techniques turn farm and garden waste into nutrient-rich compost, reducing waste while promoting sustainable growth. This approach ensures that our plants thrive naturally, without synthetic fertilizers, aligning with our commitment to eco-friendly gardening.
